Privacy Policy

Effective June 12, 2026 · Bruxel Academy LLC

This Privacy Policy explains how Bruxel Academy LLC ("Prepxa," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use the Prepxa mobile application and related services (the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Account information — name, email address, password (stored as a salted hash; we never see your plaintext password), and role (student or tutor).
  • Student profile — grade level, target exam, and optional goal scores.
  • Tutor profile — biography, education, test scores, subjects taught, hourly rate, and university affiliation.
  • Profile photo — if you choose to upload one.
  • Messages — the contents of messages you send through the Service (see Section 4 regarding monitoring).
  • Session information — bookings, schedules, durations, and reviews you write.

Information collected automatically

Basic device and usage information necessary to operate the Service (e.g., authentication tokens and timestamps).

Payment information

Payments are processed by Stripe, Inc. We never receive or store your full card number. We retain only transaction records (amounts, status, and Stripe identifiers). Stripe's handling of your data is governed by the Stripe Privacy Policy. Tutors who enroll in payouts provide identity and banking information directly to Stripe, not to us.

We do not collect precise location data, contacts, browsing history, or advertising identifiers. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

2. How We Use Information

We use your information to: (a) create and operate your account; (b) match students with tutors and facilitate sessions; (c) process payments and payouts; (d) deliver messages between users; (e) enforce our Terms of Service and platform safety rules; (f) respond to support requests; and (g) comply with legal obligations.

3. Children and Minors

The Service is intended for users 13 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that a child under 13 has created an account, we will delete it.

If you are under 18, you may use the Service only with the consent and involvement of a parent or legal guardian, who must agree to our Terms of Service on your behalf. Parents or guardians may contact kanishksasi1@gmail.com to review, modify, or delete their minor's information.

4. Message and Session Monitoring

Messages. To protect users — many of whom are minors — messages sent through the Service are automatically screened. Messages that our systems flag (for example, attempts to share personal contact information, external links, or off-platform payment arrangements) are blocked and logged for review by our staff.

Tutoring sessions are recorded. Video tutoring sessions booked through Prepxa are hosted on our video provider (8x8 / Jitsi as a Service) and are automatically recorded and transcribed. Both the tutor and the student are notified of this in the app before joining, and both accept it as part of our Community Rules at sign-up.

Automated review. Session transcripts are analysed by an automated system (an AI model provided by Anthropic) to detect potential breaches of our Community Rules — for example, exchanging contact details, arranging tutoring or payment outside Prepxa, harassment, or conduct unsafe for a minor. No human listens to or watches an ordinary session. Only sessions the automated system flags are escalated to a Prepxa staff member for human review.

Retention of recordings. Session recordings are deleted automatically by our video provider approximately 24 hours after the session. If a session is flagged by the automated review, that recording and transcript are retained by Prepxa for as long as reasonably necessary to complete the review and document any enforcement action, and are accessible only to authorised Prepxa staff.

Consequences. Confirmed breaches may result in a warning, suspension, or permanent removal from Prepxa. Decisions to suspend or remove an account are made by a person, not automatically by the AI system.

Do not send anything in chat, or say anything in a session, that you would not want reviewed for safety purposes. If you do not agree to session recording and automated review, do not book or accept sessions on Prepxa.

5. How We Share Information

  • Service providers (processors): Supabase (database, authentication, and file hosting), Stripe (payments and payouts), 8x8 (video session hosting, recording, and transcription), and Anthropic (automated safety analysis of session transcripts), each acting under their own security and privacy obligations.
  • Other users, as inherent to the Service: tutors see student first names and session details for their bookings; students see tutor profiles; reviews you post are visible to other users.
  • Legal authorities, if required by law, subpoena, or to protect the safety of users, the public, or our rights.
  • A successor entity, in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, in which case this Policy continues to apply.

We do not sell or rent personal information to anyone.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your information while your account is active. You may permanently delete your account at any time from the Profile tab in the app ("Delete Account"); this deletes your account, profile, sessions, and messages from our production systems. Transaction records may be retained as required for tax, accounting, and legal compliance. Flagged-message and flagged-session safety records — including retained recordings and transcripts of flagged sessions — may be kept for a reasonable period to protect users and document enforcement actions. Recordings of sessions that are not flagged are deleted automatically after approximately 24 hours.

7. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards including encrypted connections (TLS), row-level access controls on all database records, and server-side enforcement of payment amounts and permissions. No system is perfectly secure; you use the Service at your own risk, and we encourage you to use a strong, unique password.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (e.g., California residents under the CCPA), you may have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, and the right to know what categories of information we collect and share. We honor these rights for all users regardless of residence: contact kanishksasi1@gmail.com or use the in-app deletion feature. We will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the app or by email, and the Effective Date above will be updated. Continued use of the Service after changes take effect constitutes acceptance.

FAQ

Questions, answered.

More in our Support Center.

How much does Prepxa cost?
The app is free. You pay only your tutor's hourly rate — set by the tutor, shown up front. No subscriptions, no booking fees for students.
What if my tutor doesn't confirm?
If a tutor declines, or a booking is cancelled before the session happens, you're refunded 100% automatically to your original payment method.
Is Prepxa safe for minors?
Users must be 13 or older, and users under 18 need parent or guardian consent. Messaging is automatically screened, contact-detail sharing is blocked, and all payment stays in-app. See our Privacy Policy for details.
Where do sessions happen?
Over video (Zoom or Google Meet). Your tutor attaches the link when confirming — it appears on your session card in the app.
How do tutors get paid?
Through Stripe. Tutors connect a bank account during onboarding; their 80% share transfers when a session completes and pays out on Stripe's schedule.

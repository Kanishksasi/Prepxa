For Students & Parents

How do I book a session?
Browse tutors in the Find a Tutor tab, pick a subject, date, and duration, and pay securely at booking. Your tutor confirms within 24 hours and attaches a video-call link to your session card.
What if my tutor declines or I need to cancel?
You're refunded in full, automatically, if the tutor declines or if the session is cancelled before it takes place. Refunds return to your original payment method, typically within 5–10 business days.
Where do sessions happen?
Over video (Zoom or Google Meet). The join link appears on your confirmed session card. Never share or request personal contact details in chat — messages containing them are blocked for your safety.
Is Prepxa safe for minors?
Users must be 13+, and users under 18 need a parent or guardian's consent. All chat is automatically screened, contact-information sharing is blocked, and payments stay on the platform. Parents: we still recommend supervising your student's sessions.
How do I delete my account?
In the app: Profile → Delete Account. This permanently removes your account, sessions, and messages.
I forgot my password.
Tap "Forgot password?" on the sign-in screen and we'll email you a 6-digit reset code.

For Tutors

How do I get paid?
Set up payouts in Profile → Set up payouts (powered by Stripe). When a paid session completes, your 80% share transfers to your Stripe account and pays out to your bank on Stripe's schedule. Track everything in the Earnings tab or your Stripe dashboard.
Why can't students book me yet?
Two requirements: your application must be approved by our team, and your Stripe payout setup must be complete.
What does Prepxa charge?
A 20% platform fee on each session, deducted from the session price. The fee covers payment processing, the platform, safety screening, and marketing.
What about taxes?
Tutors are independent contractors responsible for their own taxes. Your payment records are available in your Stripe dashboard.

Payments & Billing

A charge looks wrong.
Email kanishksasi1@gmail.com with the session date and amount — include the email on your Prepxa account. Please contact us before filing a card dispute; we resolve most issues within 2 business days.

Report a Safety Concern

If any user makes you uncomfortable, asks to move off the platform, or requests personal information, email kanishksasi1@gmail.com with the subject "SAFETY". These reports are reviewed first, and we act quickly — including removing users from the platform.
